## How this catalog is used

Phase 1 demo seeds the **interventions_library** table with ~10 templates clustered from these scenarios. The mapping lives in `intervention-template-clusters.md` alongside this file.

The remaining 190 scenarios are not "discarded." They are operator wisdom waiting for the layers they belong to:

- **Memory / Aurion / signal-recognition (#181–200)** → Phase 2-3 (Voyage signal recall + Aurion in-room orchestrator)
- **Banquets & weddings (#161–180)** → Phase 5 (Atrium + corporate lane)
- **IRD / suite dining (#121–140)** → Phase 3 (Aurion in-room) + Phase 5
- **Spa & wellness (#141–160)** → Phase 2-3 (Voyage + Aurion)
- **Pool & beach club (#61–80)** → Phase 1 expansion + Phase 2 Voyage

Each scenario here is a *seed for a future intervention template*. As the platform grows, this catalog is mined.

The platform's defensibility (master doc §1.2 "operator wisdom captured at scale") lives in this file. Every staff observation can become a labeled training example. Until then, the wisdom lives here in plain prose.
