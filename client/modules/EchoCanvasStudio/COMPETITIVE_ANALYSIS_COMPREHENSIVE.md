## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

### EchoCanva Position
- **Strength:** Professional-grade canvas with extensive tools and real-time editing
- **Challenge:** Limited collaboration and template library compared to Canva and Figma
- **Opportunity:** Niche market between Figma (design) and Canva (ease-of-use)

### CakeDesigner Position
- **Strength:** Specialized for cake business with unique intake form and PDF export
- **Challenge:** Limited 3D visualization and real-time pricing updates
- **Opportunity:** Become the go-to SaaS for bakeries (white-label potential)

### Positioning Strategy

#### EchoCanva: "Professional Design for Everyone"
- **Target:** Designers, content creators, agencies
- **Price:** $15-50/month (mid-tier vs Figma, Photoshop)
- **Positioning:** Between Canva (simplicity) and Figma (collaboration)
- **Key Differentiator:** Painting tools + real-time collaboration

#### CakeDesigner: "Bakery Management Platform"
- **Target:** Cake decorators, bakery owners, event planners
- **Price:** $20-100/month (specialty software)
- **Positioning:** Only AI-powered cake design for bakeries
- **Key Differentiator:** Specialized intake form + PDF proposals
