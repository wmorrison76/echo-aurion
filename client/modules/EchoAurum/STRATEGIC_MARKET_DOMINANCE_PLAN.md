## 3. REAL-TIME GL POSTING - THE SPEED MOAT

### Why Real-Time Matters

**The Problem with Batch GL:**
- Xero, QB, Restaurant365 post to GL on schedule (hourly, daily, batch)
- Batch = delay between transaction and GL visibility
- Restaurant: 2 PM sale doesn't show in GL until 6 PM
- Why does this matter? End-of-day reconciliation shows "missing" revenue

**EchoAurum's Real-Time Advantage:**
- Sale happens at 2 PM → GL updated at 2:00:01 PM
- Reconciliation is easier (GL is always current)
- Cash position is always accurate (no "as of" lag)
- Month-end close is faster (all transactions already reconciled)

### Financial Impact of Real-Time

| Time Horizon | Batch (Competitors) | Real-Time (EchoAurum) | Impact |
|--------------|--------------------|-----------------------|--------|
| Daily | Revenue gaps (needs investigation) | Instant visibility | 1 hour saved/day |

| Month-end | Close takes 5-7 days | Close takes 2-3 days | 2-3 days saved/month |
| Annual | Year-end audit delays | Audit-ready books | $10-20K audit cost savings |

**Total Year 1 Value:** $50K-100K per location (CFO time + audit costs)

### How to Market Real-Time GL
